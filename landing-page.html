Lion Sands Ivory Lodge

Ivory Lodge is for those in search of utter exclusivity and a deeper connection with nature. Eight suites by the river provide a private vantage point to the ever-present parade of the reserve's wildlife. From your bed, deck, or plunge pool, this is one of the best places to look upon an Africa that will feel like yours alone – uninterrupted. All that is left to do is enjoy every moment of game drives, bush walks, and treatments at the spa.
