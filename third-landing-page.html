Lion Sands Narina Lodge

This intimate safari lodge, like the Narina Trogon bird after which it is named, is astonishingly beautiful and blends unassumingly into the natural environment. Perched on stilts under a canopy of 100-year-old trees just up from the water, Narina Lodge with her nine private luxury suites and gorgeous spa with a view, nurtures natural beauty and offers you a wonderful sense of remoteness and peace.

Lion Sands River Lodge

TAt River Lodge, connection thrives – whether it’s between nature, loved ones, our friendly people or new friends. Here, age-old trees shade suites with river vistas. The 18 rooms and social areas offer luxury safari living with a special magic. Share sundowners on the deck, swop wildlife stories in the safari lounge or on the fire deck, dine under the stars, and cool off in the pool between game drives. Our first lodge exudes nostalgic charm, attracting generations of returnees seeking a connection-rich safari.

Lion Sands Tinga Lodge

Relive safari nostalgia at Tinga Lodge. Nine suites, set apart on a high bank up from the Sabie River capture the romance of Africa and evoke a timeless and classic safari atmosphere. Dine fireside in the lodge, admire the stars from the fire deck, relax at the pool, and enjoy sharing stories of the day as the river breeze soothes your senses. Tinga offers a wide variety of activities on site that keep the young ones entertained with a dedicated playroom.

Lion Sands Ivory Lodge

Ivory Lodge is for those in search of utter exclusivity and a deeper connection with nature. Eight suites by the river provide a private vantage point to the ever-present parade of the reserve's wildlife. From your bed, deck, or plunge pool, this is one of the best places to look upon an Africa that will feel like yours alone – uninterrupted. All that is left to do is enjoy every moment of game drives, bush walks, and treatments at the spa.

Monwana Game Lodge

Come home to excellence, where sustainability meets exceptional comfort, creating your intimate home away from home. Recognised as Africa’s No 1 safari lodge and placing 21st among the world’s finest hotels, according to Travel + Leisure’s World’s Best Awards, Monwana has captured the hearts of discerning travellers worldwide. This luxurious and intimate safari experience at Monwana has been built with a deep respect for nature - the architecture planned accordingly around a well-established large old leadwood tree. 

Marataba Mountain Lodge

Overlooking the Waterfall Valley, and the Waterberg escarpment merging into acacia savannah, Mountain Lodge gives you space, seclusion and a feeling of freedom. You’ll feel as if you have the entire wilderness to yourself on this soulful safari adventure. With only five luxury rooms perched on the escarpment, follow your own rhythm here, with views that will soothe your soul and game drives that excite the spirit. Welcomes guests ages 16+.

Marataba Safari Lodge

Marataba offers a genuine safari adventure tailored for families and friends. Our luxury tents ensure comfort while you're immersed in the wild. Beyond game drives and bush walks, enhanced with unique surprises, there's ample opportunity for relaxation. Whether it's pool lounging, an in-room spa session, or simply soaking in the tranquil environment, the choice is yours. We keep things flexible – from dining to activities – for a seamless family experience. With our Mack & Madi Kids’ Adventure Safari Club, children are in for a treat: fun activities, an eco-garden play area, and a dedicated coordinator to guide them. It's all about creating cherished memories and sparking a lifelong love for nature. Welcomes children of all ages. We offer child minding services for the little ones who are not able to join certain safari experiences.

More Quarters Neighbourhood Hotel

The artsy, eclectic vibe of the Kloof Street Neighbourhood has spilled over into More Quarters. The spacious Luxury Apartments boast vibrant colours and contemporary design, curated not only to invigorate your senses but also to provide the comfort and tranquillity you deserve. Table Mountain is the backdrop to this vibey neighbourhood getaway, and Cape Town’s best attractions are just a short walk or drive away. This stylish collection of converted heritage townhouses in a secluded quarter is close enough to the city buzz and the action yet removed enough to peacefully enjoy sundowners on the patio. Cape Town’s beaches, mountains and cityscape are yours to explore, and we can arrange all the best tours and experiences.

Cape Cadogan Boutique Hotel

In 2022, we revitalised this historic hotel, collaborating with local artisans who infused the design with elegant touches of emerald green, classic black and white, and just the right hint of gold sparkle. Each of our 19 rooms seamlessly blends the old with the new, ensuring luxurious comfort. Our commitment to wellness is holistic. The spa provides soothing treatments, and our fitness centre is equipped with the essentials. Relax in the sunroom, evoking the charm of a Victorian home, or unwind by one of our two pools as we serve refreshing beverages. Our prized restaurant, Upper Union, is a renowned dining destination in Cape Town.

Stanley & Livingstone Boutique Hotel

Discover luxury at Stanley & Livingstone Boutique Hotel. Enjoy dining at 1871, breathtaking wildlife views of Victoria Falls Private Game Reserve, and handpicked adventures that awaken the spirit. Victoria Falls isn't just a destination—it's an experience. From the powerful allure of the Falls to sundowners on the Zambezi, there’s an adventure for every taste. Stanley & Livingstone brings a touch of luxury to the Victoria Falls Private Game Reserve, home of the Big Five. Set amidst this incredible natural beauty, and just a 15-minute drive from the Falls and the vibrant town, our 16 Suite Boutique Hotel offers a seamless blend of comfort and adventure.

Ursula's Homestead

Settle in and feel at home in the relaxed and intimate environment of Ursula’s Homestead, your ideal base from which to explore Victoria Falls. This charming homestead, with just four individual garden rooms, is the sister property of Stanley & Livingstone Boutique Hotel. It promises an authentic Victoria Falls experience with the added intimacy and privacy that only a smaller establishment can offer. Designed in a semi-circular layout, the main guest area and terrace, fire-deck, pool area, and the rooms all offer wide-open views of the game reserve, allowing you to spot wildlife, even from your room.
